Education

In 2003 I enrolled in a BA in Animation in IADT. For year afterwards I worked as an illustration, graphic designer and animator on various projects. In 2014 I undertook a Java programming course with the ETB. It involved designing, creating and testing software components using Java and upon completion of this course I was awarded a City & Guilds diploma in Java programming.

I was later enrolled in a web development course with the ETB. As part of the course we took industry level exams and were awarded professional qualifications in, Oracle certified MySQL developer, Zend certified PHP engineer and Adobe certified Dreamweaver associate.

In 2017 I made the decision to pursue this path further and returned to Cork and enrolled in UCC

In September I began studying in the Business Information Systems programme at UCC. My dog came in tow and she is a big fan of the campus

I look forward to the opportunities that lie ahead as part of my continued education
